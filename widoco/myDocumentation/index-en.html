tuff3: Description back to ToC

The goal of this project is to provide an ontological representation, coupled with a graph database, modeling a law article belonging to an italian legislative decree. The decree is called TUFF - “Testo unico in materia di foreste e filiere forestali, D.L. No. 34/2018 -, and its goal is to standardize forest policies at national level. Specifically, the project deals with article number 3, titled”Definizioni” and consisting of a set of fundamental definitions designed for the regulatory discourse within the realm of forestry planning.
